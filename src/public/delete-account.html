⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

1
Send an email to info@dikly.sbs with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
2
Include your registered email address and the name of your institution in the email.
3
We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email when complete.
Email Us to Delete Account

What Gets Deleted

DataAction
Your profile (name, email, password)Permanently deleted
Your attendance recordsPermanently deleted
Your quiz attempts and resultsPermanently deleted
Your messages and announcementsPermanently deleted
Your device pairingsPermanently deleted

What Is Retained

Institutional records required for compliance (e.g. attendance session logs created by lecturers) may be retained for up to 90 days before being purged, as required by your institution's data policy.

Alternative: Delete via App

If your institution administrator has enabled self-service account management, you may be able to request deletion directly from your profile settings within the DIKLY app.
