Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025  ·  DIKLY by Dikly Tech

1. Introduction

Welcome to DIKLY ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password (hashed).
  • Institutional Data: Department, student ID, course enrolments, attendance records.
  • Device Data: Device ID for ESP32-linked attendance hardware.
  • Usage Data: Login timestamps, quiz attempts, assignment submissions.
  • Camera/Biometric Data: If you use proctored quizzes, images may be captured for academic integrity purposes. Images are processed server-side and not shared externally.
  • Payment Data: Transaction references via Paystack. We do not store card numbers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the DIKLY platform.
  • To track attendance, assessments, and academic progress.
  • To communicate with you about your account and subscription.
  • To enforce academic integrity (proctored assessments).
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To improve platform features and performance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Your Institution: Lecturers, HODs, and administrators within your enrolled institution.
  • Service Providers: Paystack (payments), MailerSend/Gmail (email), Render (hosting).
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at nelsonkel78@gmail.com.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

DIKLY uses browser localStorage to store your authentication token and offline data. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

7. Security

We use HTTPS encryption, bcrypt password hashing, and JWT authentication to protect your data. No system is 100% secure; please use a strong, unique password.

8. Children's Privacy

DIKLY is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at nelsonkel78@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
