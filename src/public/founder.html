About Isaac

Isaac Kweku Kekeli Aryeepah is the founder of Dikly Tech and the creator of DIKLY — an enterprise platform that reimagines how schools, universities, and businesses handle attendance, education management, and workforce productivity.

Driven by the gap between manual, error-prone attendance systems and what modern technology can deliver, Isaac built DIKLY from the ground up — combining Bluetooth proximity detection, QR codes, ESP32 hardware, and a full suite of educational tools into one unified platform.

His work bridges hardware and software: from embedded BLE devices to full-stack web applications serving academic institutions and corporate teams across multiple modes and departments.
