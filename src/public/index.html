Help & FAQ
How do I sign in?

Select your workspace type (Corporate or Academic), then choose your portal. Enter your email and password on the login form.

I forgot my password

On the login form, click Forgot password? and enter your registered phone number or email. A reset code will be sent to you.

Which portal should I use?

Admin — Institution or company administrators.
HOD — Heads of Department.
Lecturer — Teaching staff.
Student — Enrolled students.

How do I get access?

Admins can self-register a new institution. All other users (Lecturers, HODs, Students) are added by their institution's Admin from the dashboard.

What is DIKLY?

DIKLY is an enterprise attendance & learning management platform for academic institutions and businesses — covering attendance, courses, quizzes, and HR.
