About Dikly Tech

We build smart, reliable tools that help institutions and businesses run more efficiently — starting with attendance.

Who We Are

Dikly Tech is a technology company building DIKLY — an enterprise-grade platform for attendance tracking, education management, and workforce productivity.

We started with a simple question: why is taking attendance still manual, unreliable, and disconnected from everything else? Our answer is DIKLY — a system that automates attendance using QR codes, Bluetooth proximity, and hardware devices, then connects it to quizzes, assignments, meetings, reports, and analytics in one unified platform.

What DIKLY Does

📲

Smart Attendance

QR codes, Bluetooth (BLE), and ESP32 hardware devices for automated, fraud-resistant check-ins in classrooms and offices.

🎓

Academic Mode

Designed for universities and schools: courses, lectures, quizzes, assignments, gradebooks, timetables, and department management.

🏢

Corporate Mode

Clock-in/out, shifts, leave requests, timesheets, expense tracking, and team performance dashboards for businesses.

🎥

Video Meetings

Integrated secure video meetings with Jitsi — start, join, and track attendance all from one place.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Real-time dashboards, performance analytics, attendance reports, and exportable data for administrators and HODs.

🔒

Security First

Device fingerprinting, new-device lock protection, role-based access control, and audit logging across all actions.
